Owner portal — the verification journey (desktop)

Three gated stages, each with a document upload and a Trust Halal review in between: register your business → we verify it → claim your restaurant → we approve it → confirm halal details → verified badge. The wizard’s job is to make the gates obvious — always showing “waiting on you” vs “waiting on us,” and unlocking the next stage only when the prior one clears.
