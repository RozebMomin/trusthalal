## Tips to actually create buzz

- **Reply to every comment fast**, especially "add this spot" — turning a comment into a real map pin, publicly, is your best social proof.
- **Show, don't tell:** the app demo (opening a real certificate, seeing "verified in person") converts far better than describing it. Lead with screen-recordings.
- **Scarcity is your friend:** "founding testers," "Atlanta first," "early access" — it's true and it makes people act now.
- **Seed the map before you post.** Make sure Atlanta already has a handful of real, verified-looking places so the first visitors don't land on an empty map.
- **UGC loop:** ask people to tag the halal spot they want added; add it; reshare. Free content + free growth.
- **DM interested owners/verifiers** individually — the public post starts the conversation, the DM closes it.
