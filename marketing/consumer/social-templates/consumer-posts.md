# Consumer Social Posts — IG / TikTok

7 ready-to-publish post concepts for the consumer-facing brand. Each includes:
* The hook (first 2–3 seconds — most important)
* The body (caption / script)
* The visual concept
* Suggested format (feed post / reel / story)

The voice stays warm and community-focused. Punchy in IG/TikTok format, but never irreverent about religious matters.

---

**Hook (first 3 seconds):**

**Caption:**

>
> Every verified restaurant has a profile with the supplier, slaughter method, and certificate on file. No more guessing. No more disappointing dinners.
>
> Start with [City]. More cities rolling out soon.
>
> #TrustHalal #HalalFoodNearMe #Halal[City]

**Visual concept (reel):**
* Quick montage: 0:00–0:03 — close-up of someone holding a phone, scrolling Yelp with a frustrated expression
* 0:03–0:08 — text overlay: "Is this actually halal?" with three different restaurant exteriors
* 0:08–0:15 — phone shows halalfoodnearme.com, the verified badge appearing
* 0:15–0:22 — quick cuts of three verified restaurant listings showing supplier names + slaughter method
* 0:22–0:30 — CTA card: "halalfoodnearme.com" with the wordmark
* Audio: light instrumental, not music with vocals

**Visual concept (carousel for feed):**
* Slide 1: hook text on cream background
* Slide 2: "What's actually on the listing" with screenshot of supplier/slaughter detail
* Slide 3: "What gets verified" — the 4 cards from the landing page
* Slide 4: CTA + URL

>
> #TrustHalal #HalalFoodNearMe

**Visual concept (reel):**
* 0:00–0:05 — text overlay: "How a restaurant becomes verified."
* 0:05–0:15 — phone screen recording: owner portal application
* 0:15–0:25 — admin view (anonymized) showing the review process

## Posting cadence + general rules

* **Frequency:** 2–3 posts per week is plenty. Quality beats volume for this brand.
* **Mix:** rotate between feed posts (carousel/single image) and reels (vertical video). Stories supplement; don't replace feed content.
* **Engage:** reply to every DM and comment for the first 6 months. Brand-defining behavior.
* **Reposts:** when a verified restaurant posts about being verified, share to your stories within an hour. Always.
* **Holidays:** Ramadan, Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, Mawlid — adjust voice slightly to honor the occasion. Never schedule generic "happy holidays!" posts; if you don't have something specific to say, don't post that day.

## Don't post

* Generic "best halal restaurants in [city]" without real data behind the claim.
* Anything that uses Muslim cultural elements (hijabs, beards, prayer) as set dressing for a non-religious message.
* Memes that depend on religious illiteracy or insider mockery.
* Holiday posts without specificity. "Eid Mubarak ❤️" by itself, posted from a brand account, reads as performative. Pair it with a real piece of content (a featured restaurant, a giveaway, a specific message).

## Hashtag set (don't overstuff)

Core: `#TrustHalal #HalalFoodNearMe`
Brand-built: hopefully `#TrustHalalVerified` becomes a thing over time
City: `#Halal[City]`
Cuisine: `#HalalDesi #HalalMediterranean #HalalSomali #HalalMexican` etc.
Community: `#MuslimEats #HalalEats` (broad — fine, but don't lean on these for discovery; they're noisy)

Pick 5–7 max per post. Hashtags don't drive discovery the way they used to; the algorithm cares more about engagement velocity.
