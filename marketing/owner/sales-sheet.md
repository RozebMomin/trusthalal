# 1-Page Sales Sheet — Content

Designed for a single-page PDF you can:
* Attach to cold-outreach emails (template B/C)
* Hand to a restaurant owner in person
* Drop into a folder for a partnership pitch (mosques, MSAs, halal trade shows)

Below is the **content** plus layout direction. Hand off to a designer (or use Canva / Figma with the brand voice + visual style guide already in this repo) to produce the actual PDF. Output should be US Letter (8.5 × 11 in) portrait, with bleed for print.

---

## Layout direction

**Top third — Hero**
* Olive band across the top (~1.5 inches tall) with the Trust Halal wordmark and tagline
* Below the band: hero headline + subhead + one impactful visual (a phone showing a verified listing, or a stylized verified-badge mark)

**Middle third — Value props**
* Three-column block, "What you get when you're verified"
* Use the column headlines from the landing copy

**Bottom third — How it works + CTA**
* Four numbered steps (Apply → Upload cert → We review → You're live)
* CTA bar at the bottom (olive background, white text): the URL + a tiny QR code

## Style notes

* **Generous whitespace.** This is a one-pager; resist cramming everything in. If you have to choose between a section and breathing room, choose breathing room.
* **Three colors total on this sheet:** olive (primary), cream (background), stone (body text). The pomegranate accent appears only in the verified-badge mark itself. No more.
* **Serif for headlines, sans for body** — same as the rest of the brand.
* **No photographs of food on this sheet.** Counter-intuitive, but: this is a credibility document, not a menu. The visual hierarchy should be the phone mockup → the headline → the steps. A food photo on this sheet pulls attention to the wrong place.
* **Footer fine print:** small footer with the privacy URL, the year, and the Trust Halal email. That's it — no legal disclaimers, no "free for a limited time" pressure.

## Alternative versions to consider

* **Long version (2 pages):** for partnership pitches to mosques or food halls — adds a page on the verification tiers (self-attested / certificate on file / Trust Halal Verified) and a quote from a verified owner.
* **Short version (postcard, 5.5 × 8.5):** for handing out at halal food festivals or community events. Trim to: headline, three-column value props, QR code + URL.
* **Digital version (web embed):** the same content laid out as a single scroll-and-share page at halalfoodnearme.com/for-restaurants/sales — handy for owners who'd rather click than open a PDF.

When you're ready to actually produce the PDF, use Canva or Figma with the brand assets from `style-guide/`. Both let you export print-ready PDFs.
